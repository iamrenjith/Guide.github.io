IBPSA England's First Virtual Conference

Hosted by the Building Energy Research Group at Loughborough University

BSO-V 2020

This Building Simulation and Optimization conference 2020 will be IBPSA England's first virtual conference. We are excited to develop an innovative approach for exchanging knowledge and ideas in response to the impacts of COVID-19.

Tweets by BsOconference

What is a Virtual Conference?

A virtual conference is a curated series of webinars, online meet-ups, and one-to-one chats to enable the exchange of knowledge, creation of new networks and generation of innovative ideas.
